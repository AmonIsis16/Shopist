Privacy Policy for Shopist

Effective Date: [DD/MM/YYYY]

1. Introduction

Shopist ("we", "our", or "us") operates as a shopping list management application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following data when you use our app:

  • Email address (for authentication and login)
  • Name (provided by you or through Google Sign-In)
  • User-generated data such as your shopping lists and the items within them

No other personal data (such as location, contacts, or device information) is collected.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information solely to:

  • Authenticate users and manage accounts
  • Store and sync shopping lists across devices
  • Improve the app’s user experience

Your data is not shared with third parties or used for advertising or analytics.

4. Data Storage and Security

All user data is securely stored in Google Firebase (Firestore & Authentication). Firebase complies with industry security standards and Google’s data protection policies.

All data transmitted between the app and Firebase services is encrypted in transit using HTTPS (TLS). Firebase also encrypts stored data at rest.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account exists. You can delete your account at any time within the app (or by contacting us), and all your personal data and shopping lists will be permanently deleted from Firebase.

6. Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase services for:

  • Authentication (Email/Password, Google Sign-In)
  • Cloud database (Firestore)

You can review Google’s privacy policy here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

7. Request Data Deletion

If you wish to delete your account and associated data, please follow the instructions below:

  • Open the Shopist app.
  • Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account.
  • Confirm the deletion.

If you experience issues, you can also contact us directly via email.
